HOW TO BUY

1. Prepare Your Wallet

Before you start, make sure you have a wallet that is compatible with the Ethereum or Binance network, depending on where you wish to buy $PAW.

  • Connect your wallet

2: Buy $PAW on
Ethereum Network

If you're buying on the Ethereum Network, follow these steps:

  • Choose the option to "Buy $PAW with ETH" or "Buy $PAW with USDT" depending on your preference.
  • Enter how much $PAW you want to buy, or specify how much ETH/USDT you want to swap.
  • Accept the smart contract call. Make sure you have enough ETH in your wallet to cover the gas fees associated with the transaction.

3. Buy $PAW on
Binance Network

If you're buying on the Binance Network, here's what to do:

  • Hit the button “Buy with BNB”
  • Choose the option to "Buy $PAW with BNB" or "Buy $PAW with USDT" as per your preference.
  • Enter how much $PAW you want to buy, or specify how much BNB/USDT you want to swap.
  • Accept the smart contract call. Ensure that you have enough Binance Smart Chain (BSC) in your wallet to cover the transaction's gas fees.

Final Step for Both Networks

After you've completed these steps, your purchase is complete. The number of $PAW tokens you've bought
will show up on our website dashboard. Make sure to verify your purchase here.
